        How to host a game

        1. 1
          Fill in the game detailsEnter your name, a topic (e.g. "CGI history"), the win pattern, call speed, and how many winners you want.
        2. 2
          Review the word listThe AI generates 30 topic-relevant words with descriptions. Edit or delete any you don't like — you need at least 25 to start.
        3. 3
          Share the join linkOnce the lobby opens, copy the link and share it with players via Teams, email, or chat. Each player gets a unique card.
        4. 4
          Start the gameClick Start game when everyone has joined. Words are called automatically at the interval you set.
        5. 5
          Pause and resume anytimeUse the Pause button to hold the game — useful for breaks or questions. Players see a pause screen automatically.
        6. 6
          Wins are validated automaticallyWhen a player clicks BINGO!, the server checks their card against called words. No manual verification needed.
        7. 7
          Game ends automaticallyOnce all winner spots are filled (or all words are called), the game ends and the final leaderboard is shown to everyone.
